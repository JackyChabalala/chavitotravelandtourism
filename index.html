Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your next trip with Chavisto's.

What is your cancellation policy?

Bookings can be cancelled free of charge up to 12 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations made within 12 hours may attract a fee based on route planning and vehicle allocation.

How long does payment confirmation take?

After EFT payment, send your proof of payment on WhatsApp and our team usually confirms within 5 to 15 minutes during active service hours.

What happens if I am running late for pickup?

Please notify us on WhatsApp or call as soon as possible. We allow a short grace period and will coordinate with your driver based on traffic and your updated pickup time.

How much luggage can I bring?

Luggage capacity depends on the vehicle selected and number of passengers. Share your luggage details when booking so we can assign the best vehicle for comfort and space.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child seats can be arranged on request. Please mention your child's age and seat type needed when submitting your booking details.
